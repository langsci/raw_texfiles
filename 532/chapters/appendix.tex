
\chapter{Examples of plural \textit{ein}}

The examples in Sections A.1-9 are presented first from Google, then from Twitter, and finally from Facebook. Within each of these three search domains, the examples are provided in the following order: \textit{was für (ei)ne}, \textit{was für} \textit{(ei)ne} with adjective, \textit{so (ei)ne}, and \textit{so} \textit{(ei)ne} with adjective. The examples in Section A.10 are presented according to the alphabetical order of the different corpora. Within the corpora, the examples involving \textit{was für} are given before those involving \textit{so (ei)ne}. Again, instances without adjectives precede those with adjectives.
