The story \textit{Casting spells on each other} was told by Yowao in 1994. The corresponding audio file can be downloaded at: \url{https://www.silbrazil.org/sites/brazil/files/yowao-castspell.mp3}.

Aspects of shamanism are shown in this story. Two brothers-in-law put spells on each other, and one of them dies as a result. The one who survives is cured by the shamanic skills of his younger brother.
