\chapter{Tuatschin-English word list}
The Tuatschin-English word list contains the items that occur in chapters 1-8, which represents about 1'650 lexical entries. The following observations about the lexical entries should be taken into account:

\begin{itemize}
	\item For verbs with stem alternations, the first (respectively third) person singular \isi{present indicative} or the first person present plural indicative is indicated, according to whether the stem alternation concerns the stressed or the unstressed syllable of the verb.
	\item Irregular participles are also indicated.
	\item The adjectives are listed in their \isi{unmarked} (or masculine singular \isi{attributive}) form.
	\item \isi{Voiced consonants} in final position are pronounced voicelessly.
\end{itemize}
