\begin{Parallel}{0.47\textwidth}{0.47\textwidth}
    \ParallelLText{\textit{Ttongo täräp me, ttongo lla da dagirnän. Obo bin a Bundae. Obo bun a ulle abal daeya. Mälläng ik a ulleulle abal dagaeya. Lla zazer ma mälläll be pätt a kälsre.}}
    \ParallelRText{Once upon a time, there lived a man named Bundae. He had an enormous head and nostrils so big a man could fit inside. But his body was small.}
\end{Parallel}

\begin{Parallel}{0.47\textwidth}{0.47\textwidth}
    \ParallelLText{\textit{Ttongo täräp me, llɨg kälekäle da gotbanegän pa koenmäll e. Llɨg a oba bägäl a täbäll a dänglläbeyo ada go\-gneg\-än pa koenmäll e dageya, emaemae pa de dägäddaebeyo. Käsre ada gognegän. Bundae bom ikop dägaeyo, bogo gotarnän pollon me. Angde ikop dägaeyo, llɨg a gotbanegän obo mälläng ik e zazer e. Käsre mälläng ik e gozerän a do yu de deyaudeyo. Pa de yu dägayaebneyo. Dompa de ade däpoaemneyo, totot dägaeyo obo mälläng ik de. Bundae bogo inu kuddäll, angde oba ttoen a gottamänän.}}
    \ParallelRText{One day, some young boys got together to go bird hunting.\is{hunting} The boys got their bows and arrows and set off. They went and killed many different types of birds. Then they came across Bundae. They saw him sleeping in a small bush. They came up to him and planned to go inside his nose. They went inside his nose and started a fire inside. They cooked the birds on the fire. They sharpened their \textit{dompa} arrows and made a big mess. Bundae was still fast asleep when all this finished.}
\end{Parallel}

    \medskip

\begin{Parallel}{0.47\textwidth}{0.47\textwidth}
    \ParallelLText{\textit{Käsre llɨg a gogezän, llamäg a nga inu kuddäll daeya. Angde llamäg a gonsämänggän ada mälläng a nongonongorang agan, käsre ansi gogon. Tämamae emaemae tot a gogezän ada, pa kom a yu ttätta da a emaemae tot da gogezän. Bundae ddobae ddobae mikutt gogon, ada eka gogon, “Abo känaebag ingäsmäll.”}}
    \ParallelRText{The boys exited his nose while the old man slept. All of a sudden, the old man felt that his nose was itchy and he sneezed! All of the rubbish -- bird feathers, burned wood, shavings -- flew out of his nose. Bundae was incredibly angry and said, “You come back tomorrow!”}
\end{Parallel}

    \ParallelLText{\textit{Bundae gotarän ag me dallän. Däba ddägattalle gotarän, käsre llɨg a oba bägäl a täbäll a dänglläbeyo. Dagaeya pa koenmäll e. Llɨg a emaemae pa de dägäddaebeyo, käsre ada gognegän doweae Bundae bälle yagyagang, bogo däba ngättma meae kuki we de inu kuddäll gogon.}}
    \ParallelRText{Bundae slept until the next morning. The boys then gathered their bows and arrows and set off to hunt\is{hunting} birds. The boys killed all kinds of birds before they went to find Bundae again. He was in the same place and pretending to be asleep.}
\end{Parallel}

    \medskip

\begin{Parallel}{0.47\textwidth}{0.47\textwidth}
    \ParallelLText{\textit{Llɨg a käsre ada gognegän, ada, Bundae inu dan. Käsre gozerän Bundae bo mälläng ik e, käsre yu daudeyo, pa kom de dapädrelleyo. Angde Bundae gonsrebeon, käsre ansi gogon, llɨg  dae pädrallag gogon. Käsre papa de dängkaemnegän a ada dägnegän, “Ge bina ma daeya? Ede bibi ngämo mälläng ik e zarnen amalla.”}}
    \ParallelRText{The boys thought that Bundae was sleeping. So they went inside his nose, started a fire, and began to pluck the birds' feathers. Bundae got ready and sneezed all the boys out onto the ground. Then he started to hit them and said, “Did you think this was your house? That you could just come inside my nose?”}
\end{Parallel}
