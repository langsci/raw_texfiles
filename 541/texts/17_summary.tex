\textit{Eramang Karama walle} is an ode written to the beauty and goodness of \textit{Karama} swamp. \textit{Karama} swamp is the location of the canoe mooring place and a meeting place for those arriving at the village by water. \textit{Karama} is also a place to go for fishing\is{fishing} and to harvest the swamp gardens. It is located about 2.5 kilometers from the center of Limol village. Pictures of \textit{Karama} swamp are shown in Figures \ref{fig:karama1}--\ref{fig:karama5}.
