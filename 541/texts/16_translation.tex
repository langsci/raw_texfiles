    \ParallelLText{\textit{Komlla nag a gämällang a dadegwaeya ami gudae deyagirneyo walle ddage menae me. Oba bin a ubi dagwaeya, Pollgo a Guzi. Pollgo da täl ik mi giddollag daeya, be Guzi da ine ik mi.}}
    \ParallelRText{There were once two friends -- thieves -- who lived on the side of a creek. Their names were Frog and Lobster. Frog lived inside a bamboo plant, but Lobster lived in the water.}
\end{Parallel}

Käsre ubi eka gontemenyeyo ada, ``Ibi zanggae e wälläng e beyarnalla.'' Angde deyareyo, wätät ttängäm e gongttägeyo. Misdae up daebegaeya ibebatta.}}
    \ParallelRText{One good bright morning, these two friends came out of their homes and looked around. ``Who do we see?'' But they saw no one. They discussed and said, ``Let’s go stroll around the bush.'' As they strolled, they arrived at a food garden. Only bananas\is{flora!\textit{Musa spp.} (Banana)} were planted there.}
\end{Parallel}

Pollgo da ada eka gogon, ``Abo ngänawa bängkäl.'' Nagda Guzi ekaklle me gontmonän up wo gälbänanatt ttonen e nyäng e dazernän. Pollgo da abo gongetamän up toko atta a deyareyo oba giddollma we.}}
    \ParallelRText{When they looked inside the garden, they entered and saw bananas\is{flora!\textit{Musa spp.} (Banana)}, very ripe ones. The two asked each other, ``Who will climb?'' Frog said, ``It is I that must climb.'' His friend Lobster waited on the ground and put the fallen ripe bananas\is{flora!\textit{Musa spp.} (Banana)} into the bag. Then Frog came down from the top of the banana tree, and they went to their homes.}
\end{Parallel}

    \ParallelRText{Afterwards, they parted ways to their own houses, but first they discussed, ``Tomorrow, we will return to that garden again to finish the leftover ripe bananas\is{flora!\textit{Musa spp.} (Banana)}.'' After the discussion, they parted ways and went to their own homes.}
\end{Parallel}

    \ParallelRText{When they got home, the owner of the garden went to see his garden. When he went inside the garden, he saw the fallen ripe bananas\is{flora!\textit{Musa spp.} (Banana)} and he was not happy, ``Oh! Who stole my bananas?''}

Angde llamda da togolag dagirnän, llayabaene eka nineyem de dandärän, bogo gonserbewän a mikutt a ada ulle gogon.     \ParallelRText{The next morning, the old man went very early, entered the fence, and hid behind the banana\is{flora!\textit{Musa spp.} (Banana)} tree. He quietly peeked to see who would steal and take his ripe bananas. While the old man was hidden, he heard the two thieves whispering to each other. He prepared himself, and his temper rose. Frog and his friend Lobster were arriving, and they went straight to where the leftover ripe bananas were. When they got close to the ripe banana\is{flora!\textit{Musa spp.} (Banana)} tree, the old man ran to them yelling and saying, ``You were the ones who stole my bananas\is{flora!\textit{Musa spp.} (Banana)}!''}

Ngattong abal Pollgo bom dangnoeyän ada, ``Pollgo, aya nängkälan?'' Pollgo da ada eka mu gogon, ``Guzi a.'' Guzi ako ada gogon, ``Pollgo a.'' Be Pollgo ada gogon, ``Ddone ngänawa be Guzi a.'' Guzi ada gogon, ``Ngäna ddone, be Pollgo a.'' Pollgo abo gonttamän, ``Ao, ngänawa nängkälan.''}}
    \ParallelRText{Those two just turned around and ran to hide in some dried tree leaves. Then the old man called out to them, ``Come out! Come to me!'' When the poor friends heard, they slowly went towards the old man in fear. The man started to question those two. He asked Frog first,  ``Frog, who climbed?'' Frog replied, ``Lobster did.'' Lobster then said, ``Frog did.'' But Frog said, ``It wasn’t me, it was Lobster.'' Lobster said, ``Not me, but Frog.'' Then Frog confessed, ``Yes, it was me who climbed.}

Pollgo da wa Guzi da, oba nag ttoen a dädme llätt gogon.}}
    \ParallelRText{When Frog confessed, the old man got very angry at them. Frog and his friend Lobster ran very fast in fear. Frog went into the dry bamboo jumping, and Lobster hid under some dry leaves. The old man searched for those two and found Lobster. Then he made a fire and put Lobster in. That was the end of Frog and Lobster’s friendship.}
\end{Parallel}
