\textit{Ause da llɨg kälsre peyang} or The Old Woman and the Small Boy is a story originally written by \JSSfull{}. \JSS{} submitted a written version of this story to the Ende Language Committee in 2016 (\cite{Sowati2016}). The text was edited for spelling and punctuation by {\TTW}, {\WKS} and {\KLL} for inclusion in the 2016 \textit{Pepeb peba} story book \citep{Johnson2016a}. Finally, \KLL{} translated the text into \ili{English} at the phrase, word, and morpheme-level in FLEx. The story was illustrated by \name{Diana}{Johnson}.
