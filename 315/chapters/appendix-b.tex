\chapter{Appendix B} \label{app:b}

More details about the individual languages are provided in Appendix A, while the seven voices and their definitions are discussed in \sectref{voices-redefined}. Observe that the data in this appendix do \textit{not} provide any information about voice syncretism which is covered by Appendix C.
