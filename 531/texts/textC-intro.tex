The recording was collected at the village of Bennen in August 2022. Bennen is the summer habitat of people living in Hewraman Tekht. The narrator, Amin, is about 75 years old. He is a skilful storyteller. His family members and a few neighbours were present during the recording. The narrator is a rich source of knowledge about the oral history of Hewraman, particularly regarding the biographies of saints and religious leaders in Hewraman from the distant past.
