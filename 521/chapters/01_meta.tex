\textit{Wot aip} is a narrative lasting a little more than 13 minutes.  It was one of the first recordings made during the documentation project. It was recorded by Alexander Zahrer on July 30\textsuperscript{th} 2019, in video format.\footnote{The original recording session is archived with the ID muyu007 in ELAR at: \href{https://www.elararchive.org/dk0601/}{https://www.elararchive.org/dk0601/}} The speaker is Patrisius Enip, and the recording took place inside the house of Lukas Autet in Upyetetko.
