\textit{A fishing trip} is a narrative lasting roughly 25 minutes. It is the longest text of this collection. It was recorded by Alexander Zahrer on August 6\textsuperscript{th} 2019, in video format.\footnote{The original recording session is archived with the ID muyu038 in ELAR at: \href{https://www.elararchive.org/dk0601/}{https://www.elararchive.org/dk0601/}} The story teller is Patrisius Enip, and the recording took place in the house of Lukas Autet in Upyetetko.
