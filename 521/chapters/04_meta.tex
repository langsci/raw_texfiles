\textit{Sisters becoming birds} is a narrative lasting roughly 7 minutes. It was recorded by Alexander Zahrer on October 16\textsuperscript{th} 2019, in video format.\footnote{The original recording session is archived with the ID muyu065 in ELAR at: \href{https://www.elararchive.org/dk0601/}{https://www.elararchive.org/dk0601/}} The story teller is Xaverius Kerenjop, and the recording took place in his house in Merauke.
