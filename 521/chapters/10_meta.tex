\textit{Tinggan Muying} is a narrative lasting roughly 4 minutes. It was recorded by Alexander Zahrer on August 7\textsuperscript{th} 2019, in video format.\footnote{The original recording session is archived with the ID muyu040 in ELAR at: \href{https://www.elararchive.org/dk0601/}{https://www.elararchive.org/dk0601/}} The story teller is Yakobus Wonam, and the recording took place in the house of Lukas Autet in Upyetetko.
