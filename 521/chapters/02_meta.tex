\textit{Kelapa ambat} is a narrative lasting roughly 11 minutes. It was recorded by Alexander Zahrer on August 5\textsuperscript{th} 2019, in video format.\footnote{The original recording session is archived with the ID muyu031 in ELAR at: \href{https://www.elararchive.org/dk0601/}{https://www.elararchive.org/dk0601/}} The story teller is Adrianus Yamuk, and the recording took place in a classroom of the elementary school in Upyetetko. Adrianus was seated at the teacher's desk in front of a whiteboard. As it was afternoon, there were no students present.
