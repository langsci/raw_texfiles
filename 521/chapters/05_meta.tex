\textit{The giant} is a narrative lasting roughly 10min. It was recorded by Alexander Zahrer on October 16\textsuperscript{th} 2019, in video format.\footnote{The original recording session is archived with the ID muyu067 in ELAR at: \href{https://www.elararchive.org/dk0601/}{https://www.elararchive.org/dk0601/}} It was immediately recorded after \textit{Sisters becoming birds} (cf. \chapref{text:sisters_birds}) and therefore in the same setting. The story teller is Xaverius Kerenjop, and the recording took place in his house in Merauke.
