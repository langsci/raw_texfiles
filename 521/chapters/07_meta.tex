\textit{Shooting a cassowary} is a narrative lasting roughly 9 minutes. It was recorded by Alexander Zahrer on August 6\textsuperscript{th} 2019, in video format.\footnote{The original recording session is archived with the ID muyu037 in ELAR at: \href{https://www.elararchive.org/dk0601/}{https://www.elararchive.org/dk0601/}} It was recorded in the same session as \textit{Diving for fish} (cf. \chapref{text:diving_for_fish}) and therefore in the same setting. The story teller is Galus Kolop, and the recording took place in the house of Lukas Autet in Upyetetko.
